The word guess game: Mission Moon

This game is developed for entertainment and educational purposes. The concept of this game is derived from famous Hangman game. The neglect for hangman game, to a large extent was also due to the changed sensibilities of society over time. ‘Hangman’ is not considered appropriate in a school classroom setting anymore. Hence the game was not updated either technologically or in its user interface. Therefore, We have develop same game with diffrent concept known as Mission Moon.
